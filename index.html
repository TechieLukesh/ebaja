About Us

Our Vision

Team Thunderbolt represents the pinnacle of engineering excellence at BIT Deoghar. We are passionate about sustainable mobility and electric vehicle technology, competing in the prestigious eBaja SAE India competition.

Our mission is to design, fabricate, and race an electric off-road vehicle that demonstrates innovation, efficiency, and engineering prowess while promoting sustainable transportation solutions.
